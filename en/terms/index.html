Terms of Service.

Effective 2026. Govern the relationship between Artem Balabanov MEI and the passenger.

1. Parties

Provider: Artem Balabanov MEI, CNPJ . Client: the individual or legal entity that submits the request. A request is considered accepted upon our written confirmation (WhatsApp, Telegram or email).

2. Service

Private passenger transfer on an agreed route — within Brazil and between Mercosur countries. The service includes driver, fuel, tolls and parking on the route.

3. Booking and confirmation

Requests are submitted via the site, WhatsApp, Telegram or email. After the route, time and conditions are agreed, we send a written confirmation with the price and payment details. The booking is in force from our confirmation, not from the request.

4. Price and payment

  • The price is locked before departure and does not change on the road, unless the route is changed at the client's request.
  • Accepted: Pix, bank transfer, cash, card. For recurring partners and companies — invoice with deferred payment.
  • For companies we issue an NF-e (electronic invoice) within 5 business days after the service.

5. Cancellations and changes

  • More than 24 hours before departure — cancellation and change with no fee.
  • 24–6 hours — partial preparation fee retained (up to 30 % of the price).
  • Less than 6 hours or no-show — full price retained.
  • If we cancel (vehicle failure, driver illness without backup) — full refund.

6. Delays and waiting

  • At airports: free wait of 60 minutes after landing; 90 minutes for international flights. After that — at the agreed waiting rate.
  • At addresses: free wait of 15 minutes. After that — at the waiting rate.
  • Flight delays are monitored automatically; the pickup time adjusts at no extra cost.

7. Passengers, luggage, animals

  • Passenger and luggage counts are confirmed in advance and may not exceed the capacity of the selected vehicle class.
  • Children under 10 travel in an appropriate car seat / booster (provided on request).
  • Pets — only with prior notice, in a carrier or on a leash, with a possible cleaning surcharge.
  • Bulky or fragile luggage (bikes, surfboards, instruments) — declared at booking.

8. Passenger conduct

Prohibited: smoking (including vaping), consuming alcohol or other substances in the vehicle, leaving trash. The driver may terminate the trip for aggressive behaviour, safety risk or law violation; in that case the payment is not refunded.

9. Delays, force majeure

We are not liable for delays caused by circumstances beyond our control: traffic, accidents en route, weather, strikes, road closures, government actions, border queues. In such cases we communicate promptly and propose an alternative plan.

10. Liability

  • The vehicle carries the mandatory DPVAT insurance and voluntary passenger insurance.
  • We are liable for damage caused by our fault to the extent of applicable law.
  • We are not liable for missed connections, flights or events when the delay is not our fault.
  • The client is responsible for documents (passports, visas) for international routes.

11. Complaints

Complaints to hello@morrison-transfer.com. Review period — up to 10 business days. Consumer disputes are governed by the Brazilian Consumer Defence Code (CDC).

12. Changes to these terms

We may amend these terms. The version in force at the time of booking confirmation applies.

13. Governing law

Governing law — Federal Republic of Brazil. Jurisdiction: courts of São Paulo, unless the CDC provides otherwise.
