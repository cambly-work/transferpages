Porto Alegre

The southern capital
as a route's starting point.

Porto Alegre is our main base in the south. From here we drive into Santa Catarina, into central and southeastern Brazil, and across the Chuí border into Uruguay. The local grid of addresses — from Menino Deus to the Zona Norte — is second nature.

What matters in POA

Porto Alegre is a city with weather swings and seasonal highway overload. Winter rain changes the route from the airport to the Zona Sul. BR-116 to Curitiba on a Friday is a separate story. We account for that in advance, not "as it happens".

Where we drive

01

Salgado Filho airport (POA)

Direct legs across the Chuí border to Montevideo, Punta del Este, Colonia. Documents and crossing format are agreed in advance.
