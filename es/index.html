Cómo se ve en la práctica

Cinco configuraciones reales que hacemos con regularidad. Precios y tiempos son referencia; el monto exacto se ajusta tras el briefing.

Preguntas frecuentes.

How and when is payment made?

Once the route is agreed and the price is confirmed, before the car is dispatched. PIX, bank transfer, cash and card. For recurring partners and companies — invoiced post-payment.

What if the flight is delayed?

We track the flight status via the booking number you provide. Pickup auto-adjusts to the new ETA — no waiting surcharge.

Can I cancel or change the trip?

More than 24 hours before — change and cancellation with no fee. 24–6 hours: partial preparation fee. Less than 6 hours: pickup charged.

What's included in the price?

Driver, fuel, tolls and parking on the route. Extra stops or waiting time — only by prior agreement.

Do you operate at night and on weekends?

Yes, around the clock. Night pickups and weekends are not at a special rate but need more lead time for planning.
