What we do in BC

Balneário is not "one destination", it is a grid of residential towers, hotels, business buildings and beachfront zones. Pickup at a specific city address, timing that accounts for local traffic and seasonality — that is what separates a "transfer" from "a taxi with an hour-out booking".

Where we drive

01

Navegantes (NVT) and Florianópolis (FLN) airports

Navegantes — the closest airport (~25 min). Florianópolis — the main hub for arrivals from São Paulo and Rio (~1h 20m).

02

Curitiba and São Paulo

Business runs and family trips to Curitiba (~5h 30m) and São Paulo (~9h with an overnight stop on request).

03

Santa Catarina coast

Bombas/Bombinhas, Itajaí, Penha (Beto Carrero), José da Barra. Day-trip transfers or with on-site waiting.
