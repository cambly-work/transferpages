Trip request

Send the details — we confirm your route before departure

To keep the trip predictable, we fix key parameters in advance. This saves time and avoids misunderstandings on the day of travel.

What to send

  • date and preferred departure time;
  • pickup and destination points;
  • passengers count and luggage volume;
  • preferred vehicle class;
  • additional route requests.

What is confirmed in advance

  • final route and timing guidance;
  • price and service terms;
  • communication format and route contact;
  • reservation and payment conditions.

Why 50% prepayment is used

It is part of organized reservation: your vehicle and time window are secured, and conditions are fixed before the trip starts.
