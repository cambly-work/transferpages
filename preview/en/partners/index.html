Partnership

Logistics for those who send
clients regularly.

If you run a relocation agency, a hotel, a real estate office or an event team — we become a stable transport leg that will not embarrass you in front of your client, and that you do not need to re-explain every time.

Discuss partnership WhatsApp

Why this matters

When you recommend a transfer to a client — that is part of your reputation, not "a separate trip on the side". We care that every client of yours returns with the same calm feeling they get from your main service.

Who we work with

01

Relocation agencies

Airport meetings with heavy luggage, transfers between apartments/hotels in one city, trips to immigration offices and notaries. We work with the family's weekly schedule.

02

Hotels and resorts

Pickups for guest check-out flights, VIP meet-and-greet, delivery to neighbouring cities (Balneário Camboriú ↔ Curitiba, Florianópolis ↔ Jurerê). We work directly with the hotel's concierge desk.

03

Real estate offices

Property viewings with a client from another region: transfer from the airport, a route through properties in one day, return to the city or to the flight.

04

Event teams and law offices

Transport coordination for delegations, conferences, private events. Law offices — meetings and accompaniment of clients from airport to office and back.

How we work with partners

Simple, without paperwork.

Booking and communication

One contact on WhatsApp/Telegram for all your requests. Each trip confirmed in one message, a uniform standard for client notifications.

Payment

End-of-month invoicing for regular partners. Per-trip prepayment is available for irregular or one-off cases.

Reporting

A consolidated report for the period with trips, dates, routes and prices. Convenient for accounting and for re-billing clients if you pass the cost on.

Pricing

Partner terms depend on volume and regularity. Base principle: the more stable the flow, the more predictable the price. Specifics after we get to know each other.
