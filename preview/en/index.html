Frequently asked.

How and when is payment handled?

After the route is agreed and the price confirmed, before the car is dispatched. PIX, bank transfer, cash and cards. Regular partners and companies — invoicing with post-trip payment.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

We track the flight via the number you share at booking. The pickup is adjusted automatically to the new ETA, without a "waiting fee".

Can I cancel or change a trip?

Free changes and cancellation more than 24 hours before pickup. 24–6 hours: partial preparation fee. Less than 6 hours: pickup is charged.

What is included in the price?

Driver, fuel, tolls and parking along the route. Extra stops or waiting time are agreed in advance, never added on the road.

Do you operate at night and on weekends?

Yes, without breaks. Night and weekend trips are not "special tariff" — but they need more lead time, so reach out as early as you can.
