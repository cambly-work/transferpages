Intercity routes

Long-distance transfer across Brazil as a managed service

This format is built for routes where timing, luggage, comfort and route stability matter more than finding a random ride.

Who usually needs this

  • business trips with strict meeting schedules in another city;
  • family travel and relocation routes with extra luggage;
  • coast-to-city movement without chaotic transfers.

What is agreed before departure

  • route points, stops and arrival window;
  • vehicle class based on passengers and baggage;
  • price and confirmation terms.

Typical route scenarios

Business route in Southern Brazil

Chuí → Porto Alegre and further regional movement with timing discipline.

Coastal family route

Porto Alegre → Balneário Camboriú / Florianópolis with planned comfort stops.

Long-distance relocation segment

Porto Alegre → Foz do Iguaçu or São Paulo ↔ Rio with stable pacing and luggage planning.

Why premium value is higher

You pay for controlled logistics, route predictability, confidentiality and fewer surprises on a long distance.
