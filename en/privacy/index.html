Privacy Policy.

Effective 2026. Governed by Brazilian Law 13.709/2018 (LGPD) and applicable international law.

1. Who processes your data

The data controller is Morrison Premium Transfer LTDA, CNPJ , registered in São Paulo/SP, Brazil. Contact: hello@morrison-transfer.com.

2. What we collect

  • Request data: name, contact channel (WhatsApp, Telegram, email, phone), route, dates, passengers, special requests.
  • Documents for the transfer (only on international routes): passport data required for the border crossing. Transmitted only to border authorities.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time on site — via Google Analytics 4 (IP anonymized).
  • Cookies: stored language preference (localStorage) and analytics consent (cookie).

3. Why we process it

  • To handle and execute the transfer request (contract performance, LGPD Art. 7, V).
  • To communicate about the request and the trip (contract performance).
  • Accounting, tax compliance, NF-e (legal obligation, LGPD Art. 7, II).
  • Site improvement and analytics (legitimate interest or consent, LGPD Art. 7, IX / I).

4. Legal basis

We process data on the basis of contract performance, legal obligation, legitimate interest and your consent. Consent can be withdrawn at any time by writing to hello@morrison-transfer.com.

5. Who we share data with

  • The assigned driver (name, contact, route — to execute the trip).
  • Payment providers (Pix, bank, acquirer) — when needed.
  • Accountants and Brazilian tax authorities (as required by law).
  • Google (Google Analytics, subject to consent) — processes technical data.
  • Border authorities on international routes — only data necessary for the crossing.

We do not sell your data to third parties for advertising purposes.

6. How long we keep data

  • Request data — 5 years (Brazilian limitation period for services).
  • Tax documents (NF-e etc.) — 5 years (Receita Federal requirement).
  • Analytics cookies — up to 14 months (Google Analytics default).
  • Marketing communications (if you opted in) — until consent is withdrawn.

7. Your rights (LGPD Art. 18)

You have the right to: confirm processing, access your data, correct inaccuracies, delete data (where not prohibited by law), receive a portable copy, withdraw consent, learn who we share data with and lodge a complaint with the ANPD (Brazilian Data Protection Authority).

Requests — to hello@morrison-transfer.com. Response within 15 days.

8. Cookies

Details on the Cookies page.

9. Security

Data is transmitted via HTTPS. WhatsApp/Telegram messages are end-to-end encrypted on the platform side. Internal request records are stored encrypted with restricted access.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. The last update date is shown above. Material changes are communicated by email or on the site.
