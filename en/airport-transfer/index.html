What is built in by default

An airport transfer is not "pick up at the gate". It is flight tracking, the right car ready by arrival time, baggage support, and a clear plan for what happens if the flight is late, early, or the connection breaks.

Scenarios

01

Arrival → city. With luggage, without the rush.

Car at the right exit, driver meets with a sign or by photo — whichever you prefer. Help with cases, boarding, transfer to the hotel or destination address.

02

Arrival → another region

From the airport straight to intercity — São Paulo → Curitiba, Florianópolis → Balneário Camboriú, Congonhas → Campos do Jordão. Without changing to "another car".

03

Early departure and night pickups

3:30 AM, 5 AM, the dark window between flights — for us that is not a "special tariff", it is regular working time. The car arrives with margin, not at the boarding minute.

04

Long layovers

Landed at Guarulhos, eight hours to wait — head into the city, have dinner, rest, return for boarding. The car and driver follow your programme.

Frequently asked

Airport-specific.

How do you know if my flight is delayed?

From the flight number you share at booking, we track status via tracking services. If there is a delay the driver arrives at the new ETA — without a "waiting fee".

Where exactly will I be met?

In most cases — at the arrivals exit (your specific exit at FLN, GRU, CGH etc. depends on the flight). The exact meeting point and driver contact arrive an hour before landing.

Can I make a stop on the way?

Yes. Intermediate points — bank, office, restaurant — are stated at booking and built into the timing. Price and format are fixed in advance.

What if I arrive with extra luggage or with children?

The vehicle class is selected to fit the luggage volume and number of passengers. Child seats — by request at booking.
