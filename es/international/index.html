What's fixed upfront

Not "as it goes on the road".

Documents, crossing format, drivers on both sides (or one — where allowed), stops and Plan B are agreed in advance. No surprises at the border post.

Frequently asked.

Documents and crossing format

We need passenger passports and trip purpose (tourism / business / personal). Based on that we send the exact document list and the specific crossing format.

Car on the other side of the border

On most Brazil–Mercosur pairs we can continue with the same car (with the right paperwork). On some routes, switching to a local car right after the crossing is faster and cheaper. Decided upfront.

Timing and overnights

On long legs one overnight is included at an agreed point (hotel, apartment). It's not 'stop anywhere' — it's part of the plan.

Backup scenario

For queues, roadworks, weather or rule changes a Plan B is agreed in advance: alternative crossing, extra overnight, time adjustment.
