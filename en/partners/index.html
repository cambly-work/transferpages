How we work

Onboarding is short, bank-style.

  1. Introduction & partnership type — hotel / agency / tour. What you send and how often.
  2. Rate card — main routes at fixed price + rules for off-card cases.
  3. Technical setup — single manager on WhatsApp / Telegram, brief templates, monthly CSV report.
  4. NF-e & billing — deferred payment by invoice, NF-e to your CNPJ within 5 business days.
  5. SLA — response within an hour, pickup guarantee, compensation on failure.
